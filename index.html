A Fundamental Pillar of Society: Music

Through time, music has survived to be a critical aspect of human life.

Our campaign intends to promote a series of Salons within Coventry centered around the discussion of how music is integrated into society.
Our Salons aim to provide a platform that generates and encourages conversation between the citizens of Coventry and Music
will provide an anchoring topic to be explored.

Reaction to sound

How effects of music are reflected on psyche physical expressions? Come to this event and see how we react to different sounds and how our minds impose structure of distinct feelings.
